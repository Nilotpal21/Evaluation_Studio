## Products and Services

### Consumer Banking

#### Everyday Checking

Our primary checking account product. Customers refer to it as "Everyday Checking", "Basic Checking", or simply "Checking".

**Key Attributes:**

- **Interest Rate**: Typically 0.01% APY on balances over $500
- **Monthly Fee**: $0 with direct deposit, otherwise $5
- **Minimum Balance**: No minimum required

#### Premium Savings

Our flagship savings account. Also known as "Premium Savings Account", "High-Yield Savings", or "Growth Savings".

**Key Attributes:**

- **Interest Rate**: Typically 4.5-5.0% APY (tiered based on balance)
- **Minimum Balance**: $100 to open, $25 to avoid fees
- **Maximum Withdrawals**: 6 per month per federal regulations

### Lending Products

#### Platinum Rewards Credit Card

Our top-tier credit card product. Customers call it "Platinum Card", "Rewards Card", or "Platinum Rewards Card".

**Key Attributes:**

- **Interest Rate (APR)**: Typically 16.99% - 24.99% variable, based on creditworthiness
- **Credit Limit**: Ranges from $5,000 - $50,000 depending on credit profile
- **Annual Fee**: $95 (waived first year)
- **Rewards**: 3% cashback on dining, 2% on gas, 1% on all other purchases

#### Home Mortgage

Our standard mortgage loan product. Referred to as "Home Mortgage", "Home Loan", "Mortgage Loan", or "Residential Mortgage".

**Key Attributes:**

- **Interest Rate**: Currently 6.5% - 7.5% for 30-year fixed, 5.8% - 6.8% for 15-year fixed
- **Loan Amount**: Minimum $50,000, maximum $2,000,000 (conforming limits apply)
- **Loan Term**: 15, 20, or 30 years
- **Down Payment**: Minimum 3% for first-time buyers, typically 10-20% for others
- **Closing Costs**: Typically 2-5% of loan amount

#### Personal Loan

Unsecured personal loans for various purposes. Known as "Personal Loan", "Signature Loan", or "Installment Loan".

**Key Attributes:**

- **Interest Rate**: 7.99% - 24.99% based on credit score
- **Loan Amount**: $1,000 - $50,000
- **Loan Term**: 12, 24, 36, 48, or 60 months
- **Origination Fee**: 1-5% of loan amount

#### Auto Finance

Vehicle financing for new and used cars. Called "Auto Loan", "Car Loan", or "Vehicle Financing".

**Key Attributes:**

- **Interest Rate**: 4.99% - 14.99% (varies by credit score and vehicle age)
- **Loan Amount**: Up to 110% of vehicle value (including taxes/fees)
- **Loan Term**: 24, 36, 48, 60, or 72 months
- **Down Payment**: Minimum 10% recommended

## Product Disambiguation Notes

### Credit Card vs. Personal Loan

Both are lending products, but:

- **Credit Card**: Revolving credit, variable balance, ongoing access to funds
- **Personal Loan**: Fixed amount, fixed term, installment payments

When a customer asks about "borrowing money", clarify:

- Short-term, flexible needs → Credit Card
- Large fixed amount for specific purpose → Personal Loan

### Mortgage vs. Home Insurance

Often mentioned together in home-buying context:

- **Mortgage**: Loan to purchase property (Lending department)
- **Home Insurance**: Property coverage (Insurance department)

Both are typically required for homeownership but are separate products.

### Investment Account vs. Retirement Account

Both are investment products:

- **Investment Account**: Taxable brokerage account, unlimited withdrawals
- **Retirement Account**: Tax-advantaged, withdrawal restrictions before retirement age
