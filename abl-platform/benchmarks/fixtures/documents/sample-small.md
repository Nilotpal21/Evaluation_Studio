# Customer Support Returns Policy

## Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, we offer a straightforward returns policy designed to make the process as easy as possible. You may return most items within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund or exchange. This policy applies to all purchases made through our website, mobile app, or authorized retail partners.

Please note that items must be returned in their original condition, unused, and with all original packaging and tags attached. We reserve the right to refuse returns that do not meet these criteria or that show signs of use, damage, or tampering.

## Eligible Items

Most items in our catalog are eligible for return, including clothing, accessories, electronics, and home goods. However, the following items are non-returnable:

- Digital downloads and software licenses once activated
- Perishable goods such as food, flowers, and consumables
- Personalized or custom-made products
- Intimate apparel and swimwear for hygiene reasons
- Hazardous materials and flammable liquids
- Gift cards and store credit vouchers

Sale items marked "Final Sale" at the time of purchase are also not eligible for return or exchange. If you are unsure whether your item qualifies, please contact our customer support team before initiating a return.

## Return Process

Returning an item is simple. Follow these steps to get started:

1. **Log in to your account** and navigate to the "Orders" section of your profile. Locate the order containing the item you wish to return.
2. **Select the item(s)** you want to return and choose a reason from the dropdown menu. This helps us improve our products and services.
3. **Choose your return method**: you may drop off the item at any authorized return location, or schedule a pickup from your address for a small fee.
4. **Print your prepaid return label** from the confirmation email we send you. Attach it securely to the outside of your package.
5. **Pack the item carefully** in its original box if available, or use a sturdy alternative. Include all accessories, manuals, and components.
6. **Drop off the package** at the designated carrier location or hand it to the scheduled pickup agent within 7 days of initiating the return.
7. **Track your return** using the tracking number on your label. We will notify you by email once we receive and inspect your item.

## Refund Timeline

Once we receive your returned item, our warehouse team will inspect it within 2 business days. If the return is approved, refunds are processed as follows:

- **Credit/debit card payments**: 5–7 business days to appear on your statement
- **PayPal or digital wallets**: 2–3 business days
- **Store credit**: Issued immediately upon approval
- **Bank transfers**: Up to 10 business days depending on your financial institution

You will receive an email confirmation at each stage: receipt of return, inspection result, and refund issuance. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

## Exchanges

If you would like a different size, color, or variant, you can request an exchange instead of a refund. Exchanges are subject to product availability. To request an exchange, follow the same return process described above and select "Exchange" as your preferred resolution.

If the desired replacement item is out of stock, you will be offered a full refund or store credit. Exchanges are processed within 3–5 business days after we receive the original item, and replacement items are shipped using our standard delivery service at no additional charge.

## International Returns

Customers outside our domestic shipping region may return items, but the following additional conditions apply. Return shipping costs for international orders are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a defect or our error. We recommend using a trackable shipping method, as we cannot be held responsible for lost return parcels.

Customs duties and taxes paid at the time of import are generally non-refundable, and any duties incurred on the returned shipment are the customer's responsibility. Refunds for international orders may take up to 15 business days depending on banking and currency conversion processes.

## Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive an item that is damaged, defective, or incorrect, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a free return and ship a replacement at no additional cost, or issue a full refund including original shipping charges. Please retain all original packaging materials, as they may be required for carrier damage claims. Photos of the damaged item and packaging are helpful and may be requested by our support team to expedite your claim.
